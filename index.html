In 2024, the DMV Vandals embark on their inaugural season, marking a new chapter in DC Wood baseball.
Formerly known as the Falls Church Barons since 2004, the team has undergone a transformation, embracing a
new identity and spirit. Having won 6 of the last 8 DC Wood Summer Championships, the Vandals are poised
to continue their legacy of excellence on the diamond.
